Quality Assurance

The strategy for quality assurance of unfoldingWord content is described briefly here and in detail in the document “Quality Assurance of Open-License Biblical Content”.

We use a three-level, Church-centric approach for identifying the fidelity of translated Biblical content:

 
Level 1: internal — Translator (or team) affirms that translation is in line with Statement of Faith and Translation Guidelines.
 
Level 2: external — Translation is independently checked and confirmed by three church leaders not on the translation team.
 
Level 3: authenticated — Translation is checked and confirmed by leadership of at least three Church networks with native speakers of the language, or a translation consultant in coordination with a Church network.
